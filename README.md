📊 Business Insights & Recommendations

This project analyzed retail transaction data to extract key business insights across revenue, customers, products, and time-based patterns.

💰 Revenue Insights
Total Revenue: 9.72M
Revenue is heavily concentrated in the United Kingdom (~84%), indicating high dependency on a single market.
📈 Seasonal Trends
Peak sales months: October, November, December
Lowest sales months: January to March

📌 Recommendation:
Focus inventory buildup and marketing campaigns during Q4, and run promotional recovery campaigns during Q1.

📦 Product Insights

Top-performing products include:

WORLD WAR 2 GLIDERS ASSTD DESIGNS
JUMBO BAG RED RETROSPOT
POPCORN HOLDER
ASSORTED COLOUR BIRD ORNAMENT
RETROSPOT CAKE CASES

📌 Recommendation:
Expand product lines around gift and home décor categories and bundle top-performing items for higher basket value.

🌍 Country Insights
UK dominates revenue generation
Strong secondary markets: Netherlands, Germany, France, EIRE

📌 Recommendation:
Reduce dependency on UK by expanding targeted marketing campaigns in European markets.

⏰ Customer Behavior Insights
Peak shopping hours: 9 AM – 3 PM
Best performing day: Thursday
Weakest performance: Weekend (no sales on Saturday observed)

📌 Recommendation:
Focus marketing and promotions during weekday business hours and investigate weekend inactivity.

🚀 Business Impact
Revenue optimization through product bundling
Improved marketing efficiency via time-based targeting
Geographic diversification strategy recommended
